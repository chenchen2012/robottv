T-HR3

Toyota's T-HR3, first unveiled in 2017, is an advanced humanoid robot designed to be remotely operated. It features cutting-edge technology that allows for precise and safe remote control, with potential applications in healthcare, disaster relief, and space exploration. The robot's impressive capabilities reflect Toyota's vision for future robotics.
