ABOUT ROBOT.TV

The Global TV Station for Robotics

Robot.TV is a video-first media network tracking the most important robotics systems in the world, from humanoid breakthroughs to large-scale industrial deployments.

Our Mission

We make robotics understandable, credible, and actionable. Our mission is to turn fast-moving technical progress into clear intelligence for builders, operators, investors, and the global public.

What We Cover

  • Humanoid, quadruped, and industrial robot launches
  • Real-world deployment and automation outcomes
  • Robot startup momentum and product strategy shifts
  • Embodied AI advances and hardware execution quality

Editorial Standards

  • Accuracy over hype
  • Source-based reporting and citation
  • Clear distinction between demo and production readiness
  • Transparent updates as stories evolve

Why Robot.TV

Robotics is becoming core infrastructure. Video reveals what text alone cannot: mobility, dexterity, reliability, and real operating context. Robot.TV exists to make that visible at global scale.
