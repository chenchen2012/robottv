ANYmal

ANYmal, developed by ANYbotics, is a highly versatile quadrupedal robot designed for autonomous inspection and navigation in challenging environments. It was first released in 2018 and is built to navigate through industrial facilities, adapting to a range of terrains while automating crucial inspection tasks.
