ROBOT PROFILE

YuMi

YuMi, developed by ABB and first released in 2015, is a collaborative robot designed for industrial automation and human-robot interaction. YuMi is equipped with advanced vision and force control systems, making it safe to work alongside humans in assembly and manufacturing environments.
